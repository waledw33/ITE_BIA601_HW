Research Team

Our interdisciplinary research team brings together expertise in artificial intelligence, evolutionary computation, machine learning, and software engineering to advance the state of automated feature selection.

Project Overview

Research Objective Develop an advanced genetic algorithm framework that optimally selects feature subsets to maximize machine learning model performance while minimizing computational complexity.
Academic Context This project is part of the Advanced Algorithms & Intelligent Systems course, demonstrating practical applications of evolutionary computation in solving real-world optimization problems.
